Transform at the Heart of Relating

How do we learn to relate? What underlies the ways we create relationship?

Each moment we are embedded in relationships we rarely consider. Each moment we are relating to our own thoughts, emotions, body, history, future etc. Yet, usually we are not conscious of how we are relating to these aspects of our life. These are primary inner relationships that impact and underlie all our outer relationships.

To create change in any outer relationship especially in sexual-romantic relationships we need to start inside.

To create change we need to transform at the heart of how we relate to self and life.

This core of how we create relationship is molded from our history /culture/education/socialisation and especially early childhood experiences. This course teaches practical methods to identify and transform the core ways we relate to life.

Body Love Retreat

When a woman is out of alignment with her body and the power of the feminine, she can experience exhaustion, emotional depletion, lack of sexual desire, lack of joy for life, and a general ‘greyness’.

By cultivating a loving, healing relationship with your body you will learn to read the messages of the body that guide you to better physical health, emotional well-being, vitality, creativity and happiness. This approach to feminine embodiment also uncovers the hidden mysteries and powers of being woman.

THE BLISS BODY Retreat

Pleasure has power, the power to transform. Our history and emotional lives are recorded in our cells; often we get stuck in old grooves of behaving and relating especially sexually. What can free us?

Meditation is invitation to enter a new time-space where freedom, spaciousness and fresh choices open to us. Like meditation, sensuality and pleasure also open up time and space, they have the capacity to free us from the past and bring us to living more vividly in the moment. This weekend opens a fascinating journey into sacred sexuality and provides the sexual self-knowledge essential for pleasure and orgasm.
