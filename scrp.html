Kabbalah

Kabbalah is a map of transformative wisdom that deepens our ability to participate in life, to relate with depth to ourselves and others and to experience the great mysteries of our nature.

Kabbalah doesn’t aim for conceptual or philosophical understanding alone. Through it’s teachings it offers a path to the direct experience of mystical descriptions of Reality/Life that are usually termed spiritual realisation; a living experience of the relationship between the mysterious source of Life and the finite universe.

Originally Kabbalah developed within the Jewish faith and from a traditional perspective we could say it’s teachings are aimed to bring us into the experience of receiving ‘God’. Yet some practitioners believe that the origins of Kabbalah predate world religions and like the ancient path of Tantra belong to a more remote past of shamanistic understandings of reality. From the perspective of contemporary spirituality, Kabbalah provides a universal message that reaches into the roots of our lives and offers valuable wisdom for everyday living.

My education in Kabbalah is through the work of the renowned Kabbalist Master and Buddhist teacher Jason Jinen Shulman. This has been a transformative path to deep personal healing and spiritual awakening. His teachings have opened me to live my life with greater kindness, intimacy, freedom, flexibility, integrity, spontaneity and joy.

Tantra

Tantra is a ancient wisdom tradition overflowing with methods that open us into intimate connection with our bodies, with our emotions, our hearts, minds, souls and ultimately the cosmos. Yes, it really is a path about the great orgasmic union with life, the great continuity that simultaneously appears in a multitude of diverse forms.

We could call tantra - the yoga of relationship. The intimacy it directs us to, starts with the relationship we have to our self. From here it guides us to expand so we can experience directly how fully woven we are to the tapestry of life, the oneness of existence.

As a spiritual path tantra is unique in offering highly refined wisdom on the theme of sexuality.​

Our sexual nature is so often overlooked in personal and spiritual growth fields and yet it rests at the secret heart of our lives. I am dedicated to bringing enduring healing and fresh intelligence to this area and to encourage the surging of deep pleasures in this realm of our human desire. For me the theme of sexuality is rooted in relationship. The vessel of love and commitment is where we can harvest the sweetest fruits of sexual practices.

My tantra path comes through the work of my beloved teacher Mahaasatvaa Ananda Sarita. Meditation, the cultivation of awareness and our hearts capacity for love rests at the core of all the practices I have absorbed and teach. I grew up in a culture with a hugely distorted attitude towards woman and sexuality. Tantra with its reverence for the Goddess and woman has restored and nourished the core of my life. This path that honors sexuality has brought me life affirming pleasures, personal liberation and luminous inspiration for loving and living.
